Se7en

1995 | R | 2h 7min

Crime, Drama, Mystery

Producer Information

Directed by David Fincher and produced by Arnold Kopelson, Anne Kopelson, and Gianni Nunnari.

Short Description

Se7en is a 1995 American psychological thriller film directed by David Fincher and starring Brad Pitt, Morgan Freeman, and Gwyneth Paltrow. The film follows two detectives as they hunt a serial killer who uses the seven deadly sins as his motives.

Summary

The story revolves around two detectives, the experienced but world-weary Detective Somerset (Morgan Freeman) and the young, impulsive Detective Mills (Brad Pitt), as they investigate a series of murders based on the seven deadly sins: greed, gluttony, sloth, lust, pride, envy, and wrath. As the investigation progresses, the detectives find themselves drawn into a dark and twisted game orchestrated by the killer, John Doe (Kevin Spacey). The film's shocking ending leaves a lasting impact on the audience.

Why I Love It

I love Se7en because of its dark, atmospheric storytelling and its exploration of human morality. David Fincher's direction creates a chilling and immersive experience, while the performances by Brad Pitt, Morgan Freeman, and Kevin Spacey are unforgettable. The film's ending is one of the most shocking and thought-provoking moments in cinematic history, leaving a deep impression every time I watch it.
